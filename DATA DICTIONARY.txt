DATA DICTIONARY – TITANIC DATASET

1. PassengerId – Unique ID for each passenger – Integer
2. Survived – Survival status (0 = No, 1 = Yes) – Integer
3. Pclass – Ticket class – Integer
4. Name – Passenger name – String
5. Sex – Gender of passenger – String
6. Age – Age of passenger – Float
7. SibSp – Number of siblings/spouses aboard – Integer
8. Parch – Number of parents/children aboard – Integer
9. Ticket – Ticket number – String
10. Fare – Ticket fare – Float
11. Cabin – Cabin number – String
12. Embarked – Port of embarkation – String
13. Family_Size – Total family members aboard – Integer
